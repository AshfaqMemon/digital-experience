# Getting Started with Sofy

In this getting started with HCL Digital Experience section, you get an introduction to HCL Digital Experience. You will get an overview on HCL Digital Experience (DX) and learn on the market trends, what, why and how HCL Digital Experience is the right solution for you. It will also allow you to see DX in Action with a few recorded demos. And you will also learn how to run these demos yourself using a sandbox for HCL Digital Experience Solution Modules Business Solution that runs on HCL SoFy (HCL Solutions Factory). This is the link to the [DX Introduction course](https://hclsw.co/hdx-intro). Below you may have directly access to each of the lessons.
