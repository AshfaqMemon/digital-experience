# Digital Experience on non-containerized platforms


Learn how to deploy HCL Digital Experience as a non-containerized application and
 optimize web content and applications to deliver differentiated, personalized experiences
 for your customers.


HCL Digital Experience is a rock-solid and reliable platform that can help deliver the
 vital services of your organization. It is designed for scaling and flexibility,
 supports authentication for security and personalization, and eases the integration of
 multiple and varied applications. Intelligence-agency-grade encryption and cross-system
 authentication keep your business-critical functions safe. Your teams can easily create,
 manage, and deliver powerful digital experiences that can be counted on, every day.
